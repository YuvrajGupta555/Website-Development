Who I Am

Hi! I'm Yuvraj, a Bachelor of IT student at Wentworth Institute of Higher Education in Liverpool, NSW. I'm passionate about cybersecurity, web development, and understanding how systems tick — and break.

My journey started with curiosity about websites, quickly spiralling into the world of ethical hacking, database design, and network architecture. I love that IT gives me frameworks to both build and deconstruct.

Outside study, I play PUBG Mobile and enjoy crafting stylised gaming identities. I'm a hands-on learner who refines through iteration rather than waiting for perfection.
